Ingredients

  • 3 lbs pork loin or pork shoulder cut into 1" pieces
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 Tsp oil
  • 1 large yellow onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 Tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 Tsp dried oregano
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 4 poblano chiles
  • 2 jalapeño peppers
  • 1.5 lbs tomatillos
  • 1/2 cup cilantro, chopped

Pork Chile Verde

Description

This authentic recipe will rival any you find in a Mexican restaurant! Tender pieces of pork slow cooked with a fantastic homemade green chile sauce (salsa verde). Serve this delicious stew alongside warm tortillas, rice and beans.

Directions

  1. Season pork with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a large stock pot over high heat. Add oil and brown the pork pieces on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add onion and cook until tender. Add garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds.
  4. Stir in cumin and oregano. Return pork to the pot and add the chicken broth.
  5. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer for 2-4 hours.
  6. Cut the tomatillos and peppers in half and roast them under the broiler until skin is browned. Remove skin from the peppers
  7. Add peppers, tomatillos, and cilantro to a blender and puree.
  8. Add mixture to the pot with the pork and cook everything together for an additional 45 minutes.
